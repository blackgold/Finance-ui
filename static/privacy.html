Information we collect
We collect the server logs and extract the ip address, device from which Digelp service was accessed.



How we use that information
We use the ip address to find the monthly active users, geographic distribution of our users and their access times. Server logs are kept for two months after which we delete the log files and any traces of ip address from all our systems. We continue to use the hash of ipaddress and its nearly impossible to extract ip adress from hash.
